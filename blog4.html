How Chronic Stress Affects Your Body

Chronic stress can disrupt hormones, weaken immunity, and increase risk of high blood pressure, anxiety, digestive issues, and sleep problems.

Stress management techniques, including mindfulness, regular exercise, and proper sleep, are essential for long-term physical and mental health.
