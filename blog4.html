How Chronic Stress Affects Your Body

Chronic stress can disrupt hormones, weaken immunity, and contribute to high blood pressure, anxiety, digestive problems, and poor sleep. Stress management is essential for overall wellbeing.

Techniques like mindfulness, deep breathing, regular exercise, and maintaining social connections can help reduce stress levels. Prioritizing mental health is just as important as physical health.
