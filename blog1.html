Early Signs of Diabetes You Should Never Ignore

Diabetes may develop silently over time. Early signs can include increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision, and slow wound healing. Detecting these symptoms early allows for lifestyle changes and treatment that can prevent serious complications.

Maintaining a healthy diet, regular physical activity, and routine blood sugar checks are essential to prevent or delay the onset of diabetes. Consult your healthcare provider if you notice any warning signs.
