Health Benefits of Regular Physical Activity

Regular exercise improves heart health, strengthens muscles, enhances mental wellbeing, and reduces the risk of many chronic diseases. Even moderate activity provides meaningful benefits.

Simple activities like brisk walking, cycling, swimming, or home workouts can make a huge difference. Consistency is key; aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week.
