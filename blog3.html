Healthy Eating Habits Backed by Medical Evidence

Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats promotes long-term health and prevents chronic diseases.

Avoiding processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive saturated fats helps reduce obesity, diabetes, and heart disease risk.
