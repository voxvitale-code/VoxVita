Understanding High Blood Pressure and Its Risks

High blood pressure, often called the “silent killer,” rarely shows symptoms but greatly increases the risk of heart disease, stroke, and kidney failure.

Regular monitoring, a balanced diet low in sodium, regular exercise, and stress management are essential for prevention and control.
